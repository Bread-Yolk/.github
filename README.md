## ABOUT BAY

**BAY** is a computer club formed by 4 college students who are a mixture of cybersecurity and computer science majors. BAY was formed on June 22, 2022 and focuses on the CTF field which is a cybersecurity competition and the Counter Strike Global Offensive game field.
